Design Principles

Cynthia Clark

CONTRAST

Zao Asian Cafe

www.zaoasiancafe.com

The Zao app really makes their food pop by creating a black background and then using a white bowl for their food and white containers for their sauces. Their bright green buttons and green type also add great contrast against the black so that they are very visible.

WHITE SPACE/CLEAN DESIGN

Uber App

www.uber.com

The home screen is very nicely laid out so that all of the information has plenty of room in between allowing for white space for the eyes to rest and so that it doesn't make the screen over crowded. It is also very simple and easy to navigate and limited to only a few colors, making it look nice and clean.

ALIGNMENT

Target App

www.target.com

If you look at the left-hand side of the page, all of the text and text boxes follow the same line all the way down. Even the icons at the bottom are aligned with everything above it and have equal spacing between each one.
