Discover All of the Great Businesses You Can Visit In Utah County!

The Museum of Natural Curiostiy

The Museum of Natural Curiosity is a fun children's museum located in Lehi at Thanksgiving Point. The museum has a climbing gym, a water area, an outside playground, a science center, and more.

Cabela's

Cabela's is a huge sports store where you can find gear for hunting, fishing, hiking, and so much more.

Lehi Roller Mills

Lehi Roller Mills has been around for many years producing high quality flour and baking products. You will see their goods in local stores. Be sure to try their amazing muffin mix!

Jack and Jill Lanes

Not only are there plenty of lanes that are well taken care of, but you will be able to enjoy an arcade full of a huge variety of games for all ages.

Traverse Mountain Outlets

This outdoor outlet is packed with all kinds of stores for your every need and at fantastic prices! If you are looking for some snacks while you shop, there is a pretzel shop and a chocolate shop.

Megaplex

If you are looking for a great movie theater with delicious popcorn, huge screens, and plenty of seating, then Megaplex is the perfect place for you to come any night of the week.

Chubby's

Chubby's is a local cafe with the best sweet buffalo tenders around! They have options for the whole family with their cheesesteaks, burgers, salads, mini corndogs, fries, shakes, and other great options.

Fat Cats

Fats Cats is your one-stop for all of your fun! Not only is it a movie theater, but an arcade, bowling, mini golf, and great food. Get some great prizes with the tickets your earn in the arcade!

Swig

Regular soda is out and Swig soda is in. Get all kinds of flavors added to your Coke, Dr.Pepper, or whatever your favorite soda is. Enjoy one of their delicious cookies with your drink!
