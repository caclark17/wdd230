The Temple Brings Peace

The Houston, Texas Temple (pictured above) was dedicated on August 26, 2000 by President Gordon B. Hinckley--about three years after it was announced. It was the 97th temple in operation for The Church of Jesus-Christ of Latter-Day Saints. It was renovated and rededicated in 2018 by Elder Russell M. Nelson after there was extensive flood damage from Hurrican Harvey.
